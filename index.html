About Researchers Network

Researchers Network is a community designed to connect marine scientists, students, professionals, and researchers through focused WhatsApp groups. Each group is dedicated to a specific research area, making it easier to share opportunities, discuss scientific ideas, collaborate on projects, and build meaningful professional connections.
